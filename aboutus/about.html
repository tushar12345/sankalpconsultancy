About us

About us

SANKALP Consultancy is a focused leader in international student recruitment since 2003, we have helped lots of students just like you to realize their studying abroad dream.

SANKALP CONSULTANCY provides complete study abroad solutions to students. We give accurate, comprehensive and current information to students and parents who are interested in pursuing higher studies in countries like USA, UK, CANADA, AUSTRALIA, NEW ZEALAND, FRANCE and other European countries. Sankalp Consultancy gives information and individual options to each and every student considering their Academic profile, study aspirations and financial standing.

Our Vision

To empower Indian youth by giving them quality education, work and settlement opportunities worldwide. To recruit quality students with institutional partnership, set up agency network & promotional activities to meet the goal of student recruitment.

Our Mission

To change the lives of youth through quality overseas Education.
