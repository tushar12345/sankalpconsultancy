Study Destinations

Why study in Canada?

Canada is a wonderful place to study and live. Canada is an excellent destination for international students due to its world class education system, multi-cultural society, abundant research facilities, innovative economy, and a safe culture. Extraordinary beauty makes Canada preferred choice for international students. Canada has plenty of natural resources and well-developed international trade networks. Canada has one of the highest per-capita immigration rates in the world, driven by economic policy and family reunification. So after you study in Canada there is a lot of scope for employment. Canadian education system compromises of language schools, post secondary provincial community Colleges and Universities which are designed in such a way that can cater to different needs of students. Canadian college education is designed in such a way that students can hone skill set, so students are ready for the job market and they will be elected in Canadian Industry, based upon their education and work experience. Then they can apply for PR. Canada has trimester system with admission in September, January and May. Canada has introduced SDS program for Indian students. We believe there is an excellent opportunity for Indian students as a cording to SDS the burden of Financial Documents is drastically reduced and the probability of visa approval has increased. Canada invites more than 2 lakhs immigrants in different categories every year.

Tuition fees and Living cost

The tuition fees ranges from various institutions in Canada. Students should be able to pay tuition fees starting from CAN$4600 to CAN$13000 per semester depending upon courses and colleges/Universities where student is planning to study. Moreover, the Living cost also varies depending upon the location, facilities and individual life-style but a student can expect to spend money ranging from CAN$700 to CAN$1200 depending upon location and amenities.

Post Study Work

Students are allowed to work 20 hours during their Academics and full time during their vacations or Academic breaks. After completion of studies. Students can apply for post study work and post graduate work permit depending upon their academics and the requirement of various provinces of Canada. One can transform to P.R. on the basis of Education and Work Experience in Canada. However, we recommend all students must stick to the rules and regulations of study and work in the Canada so the transformation can be easily achieved without any chaos.

Study in USA

United States of America is #1 choice of International Students because of its excellent academics, flexibility, funding options, world renowned faculty, infrastructure and research facilities. United States of America offers great campus experience; be it academics, sporting and research facilities. You will find most of the American university in top 500 University list that speaks about the quality of education and future career prospects students are getting after completion of their studies. United States of America offers warm welcome to the International students. As American society is multicultural and friendly, they give equal opportunity for study & work purpose.

Study in UK

The United Kingdom is a developed country and has the world's 5th largest economy by nominal GDP and 9th largest economy by purchasing power parity. It has a high-income economy and has a "very high" Human Development Index, ranking 14th in the world. It was the world's 1st industrialized country and the world's foremost power during the 19th and early 20th centuries. The United Kingdom remains a great power with considerable economic, cultural, military, scientific and political influence internationally.
